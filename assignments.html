Assignments

This page is the single canonical place for:

  • Build Assignment links (GitHub Classroom invites)
  • due dates/times
  • submission expectations

Submission (most assignments): commit + push to your assignment repo before the deadline.
If autograding is enabled, check the ✅/❌ next to your latest commit on GitHub.

Assignment calendar (overview)

  • Weeks 1–10: individual “build” assignments (A0–AZ)
  • Weeks 11-14: final project work + checkpoints
  • Week 15: final presentations

FAQ: “My assignment says Not Submitted”

GitHub Classroom submission status is often tied to the assignment deadline/cutoff. In most cases, pushing commits before the deadline is what matters.

If you have pushed and are unsure, check:

  • your repo shows your latest commit on main
  • autograding checks (✅/❌) next to your commit (if enabled)
